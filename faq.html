Frequently Asked Questions

How can we sign up for the ITSLC?

We will send out emails for you to submit your applications and hold interviews at the end of the year so keep an eye out for our emails!

How can we join AIS or WITB?

You can use OrgSync or Presence to find more information about those organizations, and know when they are holding events.

What does the ITSLC do? How are they different from other organizations?

The ITSLC is a council as opposed to an organization. That means we only have a certain number of spots and have to hold interviews to accept members. In addition to hosting company informational events, we require our members to complete projects that better the ITS program overall. This includes building a website from scratch (like this one) or hosting undergrad case competitions.

Where can I submit my thoughts on how to improve the ITS program if I'm not on the council?

You can email suggestions regarding ITS program at: suggestbsits@utdallas.edu

Where can I find more information about the ITS program?

Please visit jindal.utdallas.edu/isom/information-systems-programs/bs-its/
