About Us

The Information Technology and Systems Leadership Council (ITSLC) aims to provide opportunities for students in the ITS program at the University of Texas at Dallas. From creating networking events to case competitions, the council puts a spotlight on the growing IT industry and its various concentrations. Additionally, the council assists the Information Systems Director, Dawn Owens, in bringing exposure to the university’s ITS program. We are comprised of ITS students who have a passion for leadership and value the impact of IT in today’s society.
