Faculty Advisors

Dawn Owens, PHD

Clinical Assistant Professor, Information Systems Director

Doctor Owens is currently a Clinical Assistant Professor at the University of Texas at Dallas. Additionally, she is the Director for the Undergraduate degree program titled Information Technology and Systems. She is an advocate for AIS and Women In Technology student chapters at the university. Furthermore, her creation of the ITS academy has allowed prospective students to gain valuable insight to the ITS program and resources found at UTD.

Beth Kent

ITS Program Manager

Ms. Kent serves a program manager for the ITS program at the University of Texas at Dallas. She aides Doctor Owens in helping ITS students be aware of the resources provided at the university.
